Our Heritage

Founded in 1964 under the sponsorship of the Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA), Ikuu Boys began as a provincial day secondary school. In 1965, the local community donated land and resources to relocate the school to its current grounds in Chuka, Tharaka Nithi County.

Through decades of growth — launching A Levels in 1981, transitioning to provincial boarding in 2006 — the school reached its crowning milestone in 2012 when it was officially elevated to National School status, joining Kenya's most prestigious secondary institutions.

Academic Standing

Our rigorous curriculum spans sciences, humanities, and engineering pathways, complemented by award-winning co-curricular programs in National Drama and Film Festivals. Ikuu Boys consistently ranks among Kenya's top national schools.

Student Admissions

Enrollment procedures, fee structure, and official joining documents

Joining Sequence

Follow these steps upon receiving your official calling letter from the Ministry of Education.

1

Document Clearance

Present your original KCPE result slip, NEMIS registration profile, and primary school leaving certificate to the admissions desk.

2

Medical Examination

Submit the filled medical history form, signed and stamped by a registered government health officer, before reporting to school.

3

Uniform & Boarding Assignment

Complete kit checks at the school stores and obtain your dormitory block and bed number from the boarding master.
